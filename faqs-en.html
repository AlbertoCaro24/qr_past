FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQS)

When and where will the IYRP 2026 International Congress be held?
The congress will be held at the Institute of Engineering of Spain, in Madrid, from April 7 to 11, 2026. See the Location section for more details.
Where can I find the congress agenda?
The provisional program is available in the Schedule section of the website. It will be updated as activities and speakers are confirmed.
What accommodation and transport options are available near the event?
In the Plan Your Trip section you will find information about hotels, public transport and recommendations for your stay in Madrid.
Who can I contact to resolve questions?
You can write to us at the official congress email address iyrp2026madrid@madrid.org or use the contact form on the website.
