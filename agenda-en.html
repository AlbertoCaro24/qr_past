Casa de La Monta - IMIDRA, Aranjuez, Madrid (Capacity: 200 people)

(10:00h - 16:00h) Experiential technical visit: "Living Pastoralism"

10:00 – 16:00

EXPERIENTIAL TECHNICAL VISIT

Experiential technical visit to Casa de la Monta (IMIDRA, Aranjuez) to introduce participants to the extensive livestock system, integrating cultural heritage and current practices. The activity combines cultural stops in the surroundings of the Royal Palace of Aranjuez with an exhibition and demonstration route that includes photographic exhibitions, wool textile art, pastoral demonstrations and shearing, presentation of technological solutions for extensive livestock and spaces for valuing products linked to native breeds, completed with musical activities and a pastoral meal.
